About Me

One of the greatest things I have accomplished so far is being able to be a part of someone's journey. I have been teaching others Strength and Conditioning for about two years now. It could be the beginning of thier new lifestyle or even just picking my brain for advice, regardless I feel a sense of fullfillment.

The majority of my training takes place out of my garage. In there I have your basic squat rack, dumbbells, kettlebells, jump ropes, etc... We go over proper warm up to start off the workouts with your muscle groups ready to go! Once finished with the warm up, we of course start the routine with strength training. Normally we end the sessions with a cardio circuit but to keep things interesting occassionally the routine does get changed around.

I am pursuing a career change in the field of development. To further my knowledge and experience I am currently enrolled in The University of Texas at San Antonio's Full Stack Development Bootcamp.
